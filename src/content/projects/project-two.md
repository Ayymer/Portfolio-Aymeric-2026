I managed the planning and phasing myself alongside my co-intern, without a dedicated project manager — structuring our own schedule, coordinating stakeholder sessions, and balancing research, design, and production within a tight timeline.

Tools: Figma, Adobe Suite, and Framer for design and production; Notion and Excel for organisation.
